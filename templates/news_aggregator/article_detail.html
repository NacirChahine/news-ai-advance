Political Bias

Measures the political leaning of the article's language on a scale from -1 (strongly left-leaning) to +1 (strongly right-leaning). This shows the language's alignment with political viewpoints, not the writer's personal opinions.

Analyzes the emotional sentiment of the article. The bar shows the proportion of negative, neutral, and positive language detected. This helps understand the emotional impact of the writing.
